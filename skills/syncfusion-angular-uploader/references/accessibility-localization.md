## WCAG Compliance

Provide meaningful labels, focus indicators and ensure color contrast meets WCAG 2.2 AA standards.

## Keyboard Navigation

Ensure keyboard operability for selecting, removing and navigating uploaded items.

## ARIA Attributes

Use ARIA roles and live regions to announce upload progress and errors to screen readers.

## Localization

Expose text strings for translation (error messages, labels, buttons) and integrate with app i18n pipeline.

## RTL Support

Verify layout, icons and templates render correctly in RTL locales.
